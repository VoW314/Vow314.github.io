Fine-tuned a **YOLOv11** model (Ultralytics) on a custom dataset of approximately **6,000 labeled images** to detect and track player movement in esports gameplay footage. Built a computer vision pipeline that transforms raw video into structured positional and behavioral data, engineering performance metrics from movement patterns and in-game activity.

Deployed an interactive **Streamlit** dashboard for real-time visualization and analysis of player tracking data and performance insights.

**Stack:** Python · YOLOv11 (Ultralytics) · OpenCV · Streamlit · Pandas
