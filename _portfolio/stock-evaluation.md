Team project built for *Intro to Data Analytics and Visualization* during the volatile April 2025 market.

- Determines whether a given stock on a given day is **overvalued or undervalued**
- Uses **KNN and cosine similarity** to recommend the closest/best alternative stock when a company is flagged as overvalued
- Targeted towards new investors and students learning to navigate a volatile market

**Stack:** Python · Scikit-learn · Pandas · KNN · Cosine Similarity
