Two Discord bots developed during freshman year and actively maintained and updated across multiple servers.

**Translation Bot**
- Free multilingual translation bot for Discord servers
- Detects and translates messages between languages on demand

**Conflict of Nations WW3 Strategy Bot**
- Information and strategy planning assistant for the browser strategy game *Conflict of Nations: World War III*
- Helps players design and evaluate strategies both in pre-match planning and during live matches
- Provides data lookups, unit comparisons, and strategic suggestions based on game state

**Stack:** Python · discord.py · Translation API
