Built a predictive model using **Facebook Prophet** to forecast how much an adversary team would bid in the next round of a blind bidding scenario — applied to territorial auctions in the MMO *Once Human* by NetEase.

- The only available information was previous bids placed and remaining funds; the current bid was unknown
- Modeled enemy bidding behavior as a time-series forecasting problem
- Applied predictions in real-time during a 6-week in-game competition event
- **Result:** Consistently outbid rival teams while minimizing spend — won the entire territorial competition

**Stack:** Python · Prophet · Pandas · Time-Series Forecasting
